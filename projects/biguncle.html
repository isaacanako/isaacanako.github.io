About

An online portal for connecting parents with qualified tutors that match their needs. Tutors sign up on the platform and verify their identities and qualifications (using an official identification document & tertiary certificates). Parents also sign up on the platform and are able to search for tutors, specifying the number of children and required subjects.

Features

  • Separate dashboard for tutors, parents and administrators
  • Saved searches, allowing parents to continue from where they stopped
  • Search results are based on tutors’ ratings, proximity to parents’ locations, subject and schedule matches
  • Payment gateway: Card, Bank transfer, USSD & QR Code
  • Rating of tutors’ performance by parents at the end of the contracted period ot tutelage
  • Administrator dashboard for system overview and tasks such as approving identify and certificate verification requests
  • Tutor wallets for keeping log of their incomes and withdrawals
  • Semi-automated weekly withdrawals: a spreadsheet of account details and amount is generated weekly, which is then sent to the bank for payout.
