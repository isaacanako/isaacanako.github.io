About

This web app was designed to help users keep record of their personal gadgets, storing vital and unique identifying information regarding these gadgets to help reduce losses and thefts. Some of the information captured include IMEI for smartphones, serial numbers for laptops, chassis and license plate numbers for vehicles. Users have the option of reporting the gadgets as stolen, missing or found, where applicable, as well as transfer ownership to other users.

Features

  • Email and SMS verification
  • Bulk upload of data using CSV templates, for business accounts
  • Anonymous report of “lost and found” gadgets
  • Verify pre-Purchase - anyone can check the status of used/refurbished gadgets they intend to buy to ensure those gadgets are currently not reported stolen or missing
  • Administrator dashboard
  • API endpoint for mobile app
  • Upload and compression of images of gadgets registered
  • Integration of live chat service
